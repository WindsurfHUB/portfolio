My Story

HI, I'm Windsurf. I'm 18 year old. a passionate junior full-stack developer based in Lampang. My journey into software development began during pandemic while I play games and want to make one. studies at Generation Junior Software Developer Bootcamp is where I discovered my love for coding and enjoy it. I completed my General Educational Development (GED) in 2023 and subsequently enrolled in the Junior Software Development Bootcamp Thailand (JSD7). Here, I immersed myself in learning the MERN stack from scratch, honing my skills in MongoDB, Express.js, React, and Node.js. During my bootcamp experience, I tackled various projects that strengthened my understanding of front-end and back-end development. One of my proudest achievements was developing a dynamic e-commerce platform using React and integrating it with a MongoDB database. This project taught me valuable lessons in scalability, user experience design, and effective data management. I am passionate about leveraging technology to solve real-world problems and improve user experiences. I thrive in collaborative environments where creativity and innovation are encouraged, and I enjoy diving into new technologies to expand my skill set. Looking ahead, I aspire to deepen my expertise in full-stack development and contribute to impactful projects that make a difference. I am eager to explore opportunities where I can apply my skills in a dynamic team environment, pushing boundaries and delivering exceptional solutions.
